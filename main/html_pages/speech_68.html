February 1, 2018: Remarks at the House and Senate Republican Member Conference

About this speech

Donald Trump

February 01, 2018

Source The White House

President Trump addresses the Republican members of the US House and Senate, focusing on the unity within the party and its chief tenets such as strong borders, fewer regulations, and well-funded military. He also congratulates the members on passing the tax cut bill in December 2017.
